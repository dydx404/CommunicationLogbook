\section{Introduction}

This laboratory investigates amplitude modulation (AM) and two standard
demodulation approaches: coherent detection and envelope detection. The work is
first implemented in LabVIEW simulation, then extended to practical
transmission and reception using a USRP platform.

The purpose of the lab is not only to assemble the required VIs, but also to
connect each block to the underlying AM model. In particular, the lab links
mixing, rectification, low-pass filtering, DC removal, and modulation-index
effects to the observed time-domain and spectral behaviour.

The goals of the lab are:
\begin{itemize}
\item understand the signal model of large-carrier AM,
\item implement coherent and envelope demodulators in LabVIEW,
\item compare the two demodulation methods as modulation index changes, and
\item verify AM transmission and recovery over real hardware.
\end{itemize}
